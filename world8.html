First Hand Trap(right): A trail featuring a Fire Bro, a pair of Hammer Brothers, a Boomerang Bro, and a Sledge Bro.

Second Hand Trap(center): A trail over a pool of lava with small platforms and several Podoboos between the gaps.

Third Hand Trap(left): A trail featuring a long bridge over a pool of lava with infinite Cheep-Cheeps jumping out of the lava below.
