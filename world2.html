The first level of Desert Land. The level introduces the Pile Driver Micro-Goomba and the Firesnake.
World
2-2

This level takes place in a hilly area with many quicksand fields and an oasis filled with Cheep-Cheeps.
World
2-

The fortress of Desert Land is a building with two stories and several enemies inside. The stage introduces Thwomps and "Boo" Diddlys.
World
2-3

This level is set in an area with some small pyramids composed of blocks and platforms.
World
2-

The desert is an area filled with quicksand pits. A rare enemy called Angry Sun resides in this level and will follow the player through the whole level.
World
2-4

A level filled with Goombas, Venus Fire Traps, and Boomerang Bros.
World
2-5

A level that takes place in an open desert area. This level introduces the Chain Chomp.
World
2-

The player begins this level in an open area, but the majority of the level takes place inside a big pyramid. The interiors of the pyramid are composed of an underground maze filled with Buzzy Beetles.
World
2-

The second airship level in the game, manned by Morton Koopa Jr.
World
2-

Two Boomerang Brothers travel the main area of the map. Both of their arenas feature one Boomerang Brother on a ledge that the player needs to defeat. One will drop a Hammer and the other will drop a Music Box.
World
2-

One pair of Fire Brothers move around in the hidden oasis area of the map, accessible by destroying a rock near World 2-4 with a Hammer. Their arena is the same as the Boomerang Brothers, though with one Fire Brother at ground level. Defeating the two Fire Brothers will reward the player with the third and final Magic Whistle in the game.
